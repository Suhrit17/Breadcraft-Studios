About Breadcraft

Breadcraft is a creative sticker studio built by students, for students. It started as a fun way to design and share custom stickers—but it quickly became something more.

This site is dedicated to Ms. Bekaert, our amazing teacher who inspired us to think big, create boldly, and support each other. As she prepares to leave our school, we wanted to give her something unforgettable—a collection of custom stickers made with love by her students.

Each sticker tells a story. Some are funny. Some are heartfelt. Some are just weird (in the best way). But all of them are made with care, creativity, and a whole lot of heart.

We hope Breadcraft grows into a space where anyone can share their ideas, submit designs, and celebrate creativity together. Whether you're here to make a sticker, browse the gallery, or just smile—we're glad you're part of it.

— The Breadcraft Team
